Everything Sealed


Introduction

This Web App is used to generate pools for usage in the Mtg Formats collectively knowns as Everything Sealed

To Use this Webapp, Configure your settings on the Right. Then Generate Pool, Wait for the Pool to finish generating (May take a while, be Patient) and export it to a deck editor

One Option: Generates a Random Commander or Partner Combination, then generates 140 cards in their color combination

Two Option: Generates Two Random Commanders/Partner Combinations, then generates 180 cards, 90 in each color Combination

Raw Sealed: Generates a Random Commander and then generates 250 random cards of any color.
